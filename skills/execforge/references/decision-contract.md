# Detailed Product Decision Contract

## Gatekeeper evidence

Capture:

- Affected users
- Pain frequency and severity
- Current workaround
- Cost of doing nothing
- Cost of building the wrong thing
- Evidence quality: Strong / Moderate / Weak / Unknown

## Product hypothesis

> We believe **[user]** experiences **[problem]**. By providing **[capability]**, we expect **[measurable outcome]**.

## Operational matrix

Use A-C-T-I-O-N:

- **Assess:** root cause and present-state cost.
- **Cost:** build, run, support, current/10×/100× economics.
- **Technical tactics:** simplest architecture and measurable benefit.
- **Incorporate controls:** security, privacy, compliance, audit, lineage.
- **Operational reduction:** manual work removed, SLI/SLO, alerts, runbook.
- **No manual work by default:** idempotency, retry, quarantine/DLQ, reconciliation, rollback, escalation.

## Business strength

- Defensibility
- Compliance as an advantage
- Adoption engine
- Sunset and kill thresholds

## One-year obviousness

Select:

- Obvious
- Understandable with Guidance
- Hidden Operational Debt

For debt, record owner and removal date.
